LOVE AT FIRST SIGHT

One uneventful afternoon, Andriy was walking back from class when he spotted someone gorgeous walking across the IIT greek quad. All he could do was say "Wow, who is THAT?". His friend informed him that it was Jennifer Young. Sadly Andriy didn't think he had a chance and carried on with his day. A couple of months have passed and all Andriy could think about was Jennifer Young. That evening Andriy's fraternity was throwing a party, and out of the blue one of his friends said that Jennifer was coming and she was looking for him! Trying to play it cool Andriy joined the party and there she was! Thinking of the best pick line complement, Andriy approached and blurted out: "Do you want a shot?", lucky Jennifer already liked Andriy and she agreed.

LONG DISTANCE

What followed suit was the most wonderful, caring and loving relationship that neither of them have ever experienced. Supporting and understanding each other they watched time fly by until disaster struck: LONG DISTANCE! Jennifer has landed a great job out of college in Texas and Andriy in New York. Although statistically long distance relationships don't work out, Jennnifer and Andriy saw an opportunity to travel and explore new places together. Throughout 2 years of long distance they have had countless adventures and their love for one another has grown.

HAPPYLY EVER AFTER

Jennifer had another great opportunity to move back to Kansas City for work, and it just so happened that Andriy also landed a job in the same place. Andriy knew he couldn't let Jennifer get away, so he bought a ring and started scheming on how to propose. He tried to steer Jennifer off course by throwing out a fake proposal date. In retrospect that was not a good idea... Time flew by and the fake proposal date was approaching fast, Jennifer's expectations rising, and yet there was not a single idea good enough on how to propose. Frantically Andriy remembered the Monica/Chandler scene from 'Friends'! He made a mixtape, a slide show, bought candles, reservations at a rooftop bar and a french restaurant. Right before they went out on their 'date', Andriy said "You look very beautiful, but I think you are missing one more thing:" got down on one knee and hoped for the best!
