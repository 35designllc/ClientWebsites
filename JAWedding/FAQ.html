What is the dress code?

The dress code for the event is black-tie optional! Since we don't get to go to gala events, we thought we would throw our own! For the ladies, we ask that you wear black-tie optional attire such as a floor-length/midi-length dress, or dressy separates. For the men, dress to impress with a suit and tie. Please note that the venue does not permit blue jeans or sneakers.

Both the ceremony and reception venue are indoors, however we encourage you to check on the weather ahead of time - Chicago can experience all four seasons in a day, so please pack accordingly!

Can we bring a guest or kids?

We want to accomodate as many people as possible, however due to space constraints, we cannot accommodate additonal plus ones or kids that were not indicated on your RSVP. We appreciate your understanding and look forward to celebrating with you!

Registry

Your presence at our wedding is the greatest gift we could ask for! If you would like to honor us with a gift, we have registered a list with Crate & Barrel and Amazon, linked below.

Transportation

Complimentary shuttle services will be provided to and from the church before and after the wedding ceremony. The reception venue is within walking distance 1 block from the hotel.

Accomodation

We have room blocks set up at two different hotels and encourage you to take advantage of our group rate. A reservation can be made under the Young-Bench Wedding group. The two hotels are next door to each other, both within walking distance from the reception venue. You can make a reservation online, however some folks have found it easier to call and reserve.

Please have accommodations booked by September 1. If there are any issues with the room block reservation, please reach out and let us know so we can expand the hotel block!
