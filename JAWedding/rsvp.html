Room Blocks

We have room blocks set up at two different hotels and encourage you to take advantage of our group rate. Currently, the room block is open between October 9 - October 14 at both hotels. They are only King bed rooms as the hotel blocked off 2-Queen rooms for the Chicago Marathon on the same weekend, so please plan accordingly.

A reservation can be made under the Young-Bench Wedding group. The two hotels are next door to each other, both within walking distance from the reception venue. You can make a reservation online, however some folks have found it easier to call and reserve.

If the website says sold out, please let me know so I reach out and add rooms to the block. Please reserve as soon in advance as possible as the marathon will take up many reservations across the city! If you prefer to stay at a different hotel or AirBNB, that is totally fine!

Transportation

Transportation will be provided from the Canopy Hotel to the Ceremony, and from the Ceremony to the Reception venue (1 block over from the Hotel) so please ensure you are able to find other means of transportation if you are not going to be taking the shuttle!
